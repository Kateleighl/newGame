Welcome to Seek & Drop

DIRECTIONS

English 1. Each player gets 4 cards 2. Place the deck of cards in the center and draw the top card to start the game. 3. Looking at the cards in your hand, try to find the matching symbol. Then place it on top of the face up card deck. 4. After your turn, you draw one card. 5. The goal of the game is to get rid of all the cards.

SUMMARY

1. Players 3-10 2. You have to let one other person go inbetween skips. 3. You can skip a person or multiple people. If you do, you have to draw the number of pwople you skip. If there is no cards left in the deck, there is no penalty. FOR EXAMPLE: if you skip one person, you pick up two cars because you skipped one person and played your turn. 4. If you skip a person, then the next turn is the person sitting to your left. 5. You can not put down multiple cards at once on your turn.
